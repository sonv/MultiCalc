        If $\theta$ is the angle between the vectors $\textbf{u}$ and $\textbf{v}$ in $\mathbb{R}^2$, then

        \textbf{u}\cdot \textbf{v} = |\textbf{u}|| \textbf{v}| \cos \theta \,.
   \end{equation*}
